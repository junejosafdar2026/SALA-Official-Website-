About Shah Abdul Latif Academy

Named after the great Sufi poet and philosopher Shah Abdul Latif Bhittai, our academy carries forward his legacy of wisdom, knowledge and enlightenment for the modern generation.

✿ ❀ ✿

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible education in Mathematics, English, Science and Technology to college students across Pakistan and beyond — completely free of charge.

Shah Abdul Latif Academy was founded with a simple belief: every student deserves access to quality educational resources, regardless of their financial background. Our platform offers interactive quizzes, leaderboards, progress tracking, and a community discussion space — all at zero cost.

Inspired by Shah Abdul Latif Bhittai's philosophy that knowledge is the true light of the soul, we strive to nurture curious minds and help students reach their highest potential. Whether you're studying Calculus, mastering English grammar, or exploring new scientific concepts — we are here to support your journey.
