Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Shah Abdul Latif Academy ("we", "our", "the Academy"), founded by Safdar Junejo, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Registration data: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, quiz scores, XP earned, and time spent on the platform.
  • Comments: Any comments or messages you post in our discussion sections.
  • Technical data: Browser type, referrer URL, and basic device information for analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your student account.
  • To track your quiz progress and XP for the leaderboard.
  • To display your comments in the community discussion sections.
  • To allow the Academy administrator (Safdar Junejo) to monitor site usage and ensure a safe environment.
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns.

4. Admin Monitoring

Please be aware that the Academy administrator has access to visitor logs, registration data, quiz activity, and all comments posted on the platform. This is to ensure academic integrity, site safety, and to help improve the educational experience. Your activity on this site is visible to the administrator.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is not transmitted to any external servers. Your data remains on your device. Clearing your browser's storage will delete all account information.

6. Cookies

This website uses localStorage (not cookies) to store session information and preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for college students (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

8. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account data by clearing your browser's localStorage.
  • You can request deletion of your comments by contacting us.
  • You can stop using the platform at any time.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

By using Shah Abdul Latif Academy, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Shah Abdul Latif Academy — By Safdar Junejo.
